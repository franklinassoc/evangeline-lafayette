Upcomming Public Open House

A concluding open house is being scheduled during September of 2024 to provide an opportunity for the project team to deliver a summary presentation describing the Evangeline Corridor Economic Action Plan, to discuss ideas and exhibits face-to-face, and to receive a final round of public input. The plan will have been through preliminary review by Lafayette Consolidated Government (LCG) and the Evangeline Thruway Redevelopment Team (ETRT) among others.

You are invited to attend this open house event celebrating the conclusion of the Economic Action Planning process. Check back for more information.
