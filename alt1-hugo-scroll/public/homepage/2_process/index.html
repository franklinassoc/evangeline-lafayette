Process

The planning process commenced in November 2023 and will concluded during the fall of 2024. It consisted of several interrelated work streams as follows: 

  1. Identify opportunities: The project team analyzed a wide range of data related to existing conditions, engaged the community and stakeholders, and developed a list of project recommendations.
  2. Measure economic impact: The team conducted analysis to quantify and specify indicators related to economic growth and equitable development for the recommended projects.
  3. Plan for actions: The team produced project specifications for the recommended actions and created a roadmap for implementation
  4. Produce the marketing plan: The team produced communication materials for key catalytic development sites.
